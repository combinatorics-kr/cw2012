Registration

    If you want to participate, please fill out the registration form at the bottom of this page and email to cw2012@combinatorics.kr until July 20, 2012.

    In particular, if you want to give a contributed talk, please submit the title and the long abstract (over half page) of your talk when you send a registration. Invited talks are for 50 minutes each and contributed talks are for 25 minutes each.

    We plan to reserve hotels near Gwangju station, which is 10 minutes distance from CNU. It will cost 50,000 Won per night. We will give more information later.

    We are going to support the accomodation of graduate students who regestered on or before July 20 as long as our budget allows. We encourage students to come to the workshop
