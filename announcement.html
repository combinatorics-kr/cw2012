1st Announcement (July 5, 2012)

The 9th, "2012 Combinatorics Workshop" will take place in Gwangju at Chonnam National University during August 9-10.

Here is the workshop homepage: 

http://www.math.chonnam.ac.kr/swkim/cw2012/

If you want to participate, please fill out the registration form on the workshop webpage and email to cw2012@combinatorics.kr until July 20, 2012.
In particular, if you want to give a contributed talk, please include the title and the abstract when you send a registration form.
Invited talks are for 50 minutes each and contributed talks are for 25 minutes each.

We will update information on the workshop such as invited speakers, registration and accommodations.

If you have any question, please email to cw2012@combinatorics.kr.
And please forward this email to anyone who might be interested. 
Thank you very much.

Best wishes,

http://www.math.chonnam.ac.kr/swkim/cw2012/
